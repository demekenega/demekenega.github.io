Artistic

Historical Costumes

Originally, Eskista dancers adorned animal skins, evolving into cloth-based outfits reflecting traditional designs.

Costume Evolution

Modern costumes for the Amhara and Tigray regions feature embroidered and colorful designs, blending tradition with contemporary influences.

Instruments

The Kebero drum is central to the Eskista dance, providing a strong rhythmic foundation for the dancers.

Contemporary Interpretation & Media Influence

Eskista has been incorporated into modern media, with artists and musicians showcasing the dance to a global audience.
